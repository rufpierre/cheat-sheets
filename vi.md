modes
===
command mode: **escape**  
insert: **escape + i**

moves
===
down: **j**, up: **k**, left: **j**, right: **l**  
move one word: **w**, move one word backwards: **b**  
end of line: **$** 

edition
===
cut line: **dd**  
copy line: **yy**  
paste after: **p**, before: **P**    
delete one character on right (like *suppr* key): **x** or **suppr**  
delete one character on left (like *backspace* key): **X**  

misc
===
undo: **u**, redo: **crtl+r**
