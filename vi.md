modes
===
command mode: **escape**  
insert: **escape + i**

moves
===
down: **j**, up: **k**, left: **j**, right: **l**  
move one word: **w**, move one word backwards: **b**  
end of line: **$** 

edition
===
delete line: **dd**  
copy line: **yy**  
paste after: **p**  
delete one character: **x** or **suppr**  

misc
===
undo: **u**, redo: **crtl+r**
